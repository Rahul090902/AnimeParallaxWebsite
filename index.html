Anime is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from or associated with Japan. The word anime is the pronunciation of animation in Japanese, where this term references all animation. Outside Japan, anime refers specifically to animation from Japan or as a Japanese-disseminated animation style often characterized by colorful graphics, vibrant characters and fantastical themes.

Explore Anime

Anime is most often associated with science fiction and fantasy genres. Some of the most popular anime series include Dragonball Z, Naruto and Fullmetal Alchemist. In addition to TV shows and movies, anime includes video games that tell stories through action. Anime has been around since the early 20th century, but it wasn't recognized as an art form outside of Japan until after World War II.
